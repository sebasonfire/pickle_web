Merchant Cash Advance

Fast, flexible working capital for your business.

Discover our solutions

A Merchant Cash Advance (MCA) provides your business with a lump sum of capital in exchange for a small percentage of future sales. It’s a streamlined solution designed for speed and flexibility—ideal when opportunities can’t wait.

Funding amounts

From $10,000 up to $5,000,000.

Terms

Repayment terms up to 12 months.

Repayment frequency

Daily or Weekly payments.

Repayment method

Via ACH debits.

How it works
  1. Apply online: complete a short application with basic business info.
  2. Provide statements: recent bank statements help us size the advance.
  3. Get funded: receive a lump sum directly to your business bank account.
  4. Repay with sales: we debit small, regular amounts via ACH (daily/weekly) until satisfied.
Program details
  • Funding amounts from $10,000 up to $5,000,000.
  • Repayment terms up to 12 months.
  • Repayment frequency: Daily or Weekly.
  • Repayment method: ACH debits.
Eligibility
  • 3+ months in business (ideal 6–12+).
  • Consistent monthly revenue and active business bank account.
  • U.S.-based business and owner over 18.
Common use cases
  • Inventory purchases
  • Marketing & growth
  • Cash-flow bridge
  • Equipment & repairs
  • Seasonal demand
  • Unexpected opportunities
